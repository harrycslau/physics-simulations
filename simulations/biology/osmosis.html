Osmosis Simulator

Observe how water molecules cross a differentially permeable membrane

Experiment Setup

Pure water High concentration
Water potential: Very high
Pure water High concentration
Water potential: Low

Legend

Water molecule (permeable)
Solute molecule (blocked by membrane)
Differentially permeable membrane
Net movement: Dynamic equilibrium (net flow ≈ 0)
Observation focus:
  • The dashed center line represents the differentially permeable membrane.
  • Red solute particles are too large to pass through the tiny pores.
  • Blue water molecules are small enough to move freely.
  • Water flows from higher water potential (less solute) to lower water potential (more solute).
